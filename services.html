    Our Services

    We specialize in the recycling of computer parts,includes:
    • RAM - Efficient and environmentally friendly processing of RAM chips.
    • Hard Drives - Secure data destruction and recycling of hard drives.
    • Motherboard Components - Extraction and proper disposal of valuable components.

    We provide a range of professional services to help manage your electronic waste (e-waste) securely and sustainably. Below are the key services we offer:

    Data Destruction

    We ensure the complete destruction of data stored on your electronic devices. Using industry-leading software and hardware solutions, we can securely erase data from hard drives, mobile phones, SSDs, and more, ensuring your confidential information is never exposed. Our data destruction service includes:

    • Data Wiping (DoD 5220.22-M compliant)
    • Physical Destruction (shredding, crushing, and degaussing)
    • Certificate of Data Destruction for compliance and peace of mind

    E-Waste Recycling

    We offer environmentally friendly and sustainable electronic waste recycling services. Our recycling process adheres to industry standards and local regulations. We focus on:

    • Recycling of computers, phones, monitors, and other e-waste
    • Extracting valuable materials like gold, copper, and rare earth metals
    • Proper disposal of hazardous materials such as lead, mercury, and cadmium

    Asset Recovery

    If your business is upgrading its hardware, we offer asset recovery services to help recover any usable components and reduce overall disposal costs. This service includes:

    • Evaluation of hardware for reuse and resale
    • Refurbishment and repurposing of old devices
    • Guaranteed secure handling of sensitive information

    Secure Logistics and Collection

    We offer on-site collection and secure transportation for your e-waste. Our team provides safe handling and transportation to ensure that your devices are kept secure throughout the process.

    Consulting Services

    We help businesses build effective e-waste management policies and comply with regulations. Our consultancy services include:

    • Advice on creating an e-waste disposal plan
    • Training on secure data destruction practices
    • Audit of current e-waste disposal practices
