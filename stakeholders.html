Our Stakeholders

We work with a wide range of stakeholders to ensure our operations are effective and compliant. Below is a list of our key partners and stakeholders:

Investors

Major investor rights refer to the contractual privileges and protections granted to investors, particularly those who provide significant funding to a company by hitting ownership thresholds or taking over specific percentages of particular funding rounds.

1. Government Agencies

We collaborate with local and national government bodies to ensure we follow environmental laws and regulations related to electronic waste disposal and recycling. Our partnerships help us remain compliant with the latest regulations.

2. Environmental NGOs

We partner with environmental organizations to promote the importance of proper e-waste recycling and to support global sustainability initiatives.

3. Corporate Clients

Many businesses rely on us for secure data destruction and to ensure responsible e-waste disposal. We work with companies across a variety of industries including IT, healthcare, education, and finance.

4. Suppliers and Manufacturers

Our partnerships with electronics manufacturers and suppliers allow us to recycle and repurpose electronic devices. We collaborate with these organizations to recover valuable materials like gold, silver, and copper. < a href="https://www.made-in-china.com/manufacturers/iwb.html">

5. Research Institutions

We collaborate with research institutions to advance sustainable recycling technologies and improve the efficiency of e-waste recycling processes.
